Glascow Coma Scale
Assessment of Mental Status
Eyes: 1. Does not open. 2. Opens with painful stimuli. 3. Opens in response to voice. 4. Opens spontaneously.

Verbal: 1. Makes no sound. 2. Incomprehensible sounds. 3. Utters inappropriate words. 4. Confused, disoriented. 5. Oriented, converses normally.

Motor: 1. No movement. 2. Extension to painful stimuli. 3. Abnormal flexion to painful stimuli. 4. Withdrawl from painful stimuli. 5. Localizes painful stimuli. 6. Follows commands.
